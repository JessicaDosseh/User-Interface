Der Berliner

Is a collection of point and shoot images taken by an amateur photographer. These photographs are based on the subconscious. A compilation of imperfections and blind perspectives; nevertheless, we can still refer to it as Art. What is Art? Art is endless. Art goes beyond our individual skills. Art bends and shifts. Art transcends time. Art regardless of the form lies in the eyes of the beholder. This photo journal is inspired by the act of letting go, the start of new beginnings, and the reliance on outside forces to guide the process, to create the work, to reveal the perspective. It may be foolish to go about creating work this way; nevertheless, sometimes it's necessary.
